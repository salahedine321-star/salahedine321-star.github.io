SalatVault — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SalatVault does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your privacy is sacred to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

SalatVault does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal details.

Location Data

SalatVault uses your device's location solely to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Your location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Prayer tracking records and streaks
  • Quran reading progress and bookmarks
  • Thawab coin balance and store purchases
  • App preferences and settings
  • Tasbih counter data

This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Advertising

SalatVault displays ads through Google AdMob to keep the app affordable. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. We offer a one-time in-app purchase to remove all ads permanently.

We will never sell your data to advertisers, governments, or any third party.

In-App Purchases

SalatVault offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

SalatVault does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Third-Party Services

SalatVault uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements
  • Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases
  • Islamic audio CDN — for streaming Quran recitation

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: salatvault@gmail.com
