BloxVault — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

BloxVault does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your privacy matters to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

BloxVault does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal details to use the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Daily login streak and check-in history
  • BloxCoins balance and challenge progress
  • Saved games and bookmarked guides
  • Quiz scores and history
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Advertising

BloxVault displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings under Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

We do not sell your data to advertisers or any third party.

Remote Configuration

BloxVault uses a remote configuration service to manage app settings such as enabling or disabling features, updating content, and displaying announcements. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

BloxVault uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements
  • Google User Messaging Platform — for consent management
  • JSONBin — for remote app configuration (no user data transmitted)

Children's Privacy

BloxVault does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and complies with COPPA guidelines.
